## Exterior Architecture

### Description
A futuristic colosseum -- circular in plan, rising in tiered steps, open at the top. The Arena is wide rather than tall, occupying the largest ground footprint of any district building. Rising tiers of seating are visible from the outside through gaps in the facade, communicating spectacle and competition. The open roof is the defining silhouette feature: where every other building is enclosed, the Arena exposes its interior to the sky, allowing the energy pipeline to enter dramatically from above as a lightning bolt that strikes the apex.

A grand entrance archway on the primary face uses oversized proportions -- twice the height of a normal door -- flanked by victory pillars at the four cardinal points. These pillars are tall narrow columns topped with flame/energy beacons that glow coral and gold. A leaderboard display wraps around a section of the exterior wall: a curved illuminated panel showing rank positions and scores. The approach to the building features a competitor walkway -- a raised ceremonial path leading to the grand entrance.

### Key Architectural Elements
- Circular colosseum main structure: 8 stories of rising tiers, wide footprint
- Rising tiered seating visible through facade gaps (stepped rings getting higher toward rim)
- Open roof: no ceiling, sky visible from within (key silhouette feature)
- Grand entrance archway: oversized proportions, 2x normal height
- Victory pillars at 4 cardinal points: tall narrow columns with energy beacons at top
- Flame/energy beacons at rim: coral + gold glow at each pillar top (4 beacons)
- Leaderboard display on exterior wall: curved illuminated panel wrapping 90 degrees
- Competitor walkway: raised ceremonial approach path to entrance
- Energy lightning bolt entry point: open apex where pipeline energy arrives
- Structural rim ring at top: thick torus defining the open-roof edge

### Silhouette Markers
Colosseum with open top and rising tiered seating -- unmistakable arena shape. The only circular building with an open roof. The four victory pillars with beacons at cardinal points create a cross pattern when viewed from above. Wide and short compared to every tower in the city.

## Interior -- Competition Floor

### Description
The arena interior is a bowl-shaped space with tiered seating rising on all sides and open sky above. The competition floor at the center is a flat circular platform surrounded by achievement towers -- vertical pillars of varying heights that glow at different intensities based on achievement level, creating an uneven luminous forest at the arena perimeter. The central feature is a 3D holographic leaderboard: a cylindrical projection showing rank positions with animated shifts as competitors move up or down.

Head-to-head competition zones are marked on the floor as paired circles connected by a challenge line. Team challenge spaces seat 5v5 groups on opposite sides of raised platforms. Holographic challenge cards orbit the central leaderboard -- flat translucent rectangles rotating slowly at various heights. Warm light blooms (NOT confetti) celebrate milestones -- soft expanding spheres of golden light. Progression monuments take the form of ascending light staircases: short stepped structures where each step glows brighter than the last.

### Focal Point
The central 3D holographic leaderboard -- a cylindrical projection approximately 4m diameter and 6m tall at the dead center of the competition floor, showing animated rank positions shifting in real time.

### Supporting Props (7 items)
- Achievement towers: 8-10 vertical pillars at arena perimeter, varying heights, emissive glow
- Head-to-head competition zones: 2 paired circle markings on floor with connecting line
- Team challenge platforms: 2 raised rectangular platforms with seating for 5 per side
- Orbiting challenge cards: 4-6 flat translucent rectangles rotating around leaderboard
- Milestone light bloom emitters: 2 spherical forms for golden celebration effect
- Progression monuments: 2 ascending stepped structures (4-5 steps each, increasing glow)
- Tiered seating rows: concentric rings rising from floor to rim (simplified wedge geometry)

### Light Empties
- light_0: Directly above competition floor center (open sky) -- natural/energy top-down key
- light_1: Behind leaderboard cylinder -- coral backlight for holographic projection glow
- light_2: At achievement tower perimeter -- warm gold accent lighting from tower glow

### Camera Target
Center of the competition floor at human chest height, looking slightly upward to capture the holographic leaderboard, with achievement towers visible at the perimeter, tiered seating rising behind, and open sky above.
