## Exterior Architecture

### Description
The Recovery Dreamscape is the most ethereal structure in Balencia City -- a cloud-like organic form hovering above a mirror-still lake on pillars of soft indigo light. There are no sharp edges anywhere on this building. Every surface curves, every corner is rounded, every transition is smooth. The form looks like a cluster of soft cloud masses merged together, with a translucent outer shell that glows faintly from within at muted indigo intensity.

The building floats on indigo light pillars -- not solid structural columns, but vertical beams of soft light that emerge from the lake surface and support the cloud form above. The mirror-still lake below doubles the structure through reflection. No visible floor separations exist; the interior volume is one continuous organic space wrapped in layered translucent shells. Faint star-like lights dot the surface like embedded jewels. Gentle trailing wisps extend from the edges of the form -- thin tapering tendrils that fade into nothing, as if the building is slowly dissolving into the air. An aurora-like glow emanates from within, visible through the translucent shells in shifting muted indigo and silver.

### Key Architectural Elements
- Cloud-like main form: organic, no sharp edges, merged mass shapes (2-3 lobes)
- Indigo light pillars: 4-6 vertical light beams from lake to building (emissive cylinders)
- Mirror-still lake surface below: flat reflective plane (distinct from Yoga lake)
- Soft translucent shell layers: 2 nested organic shells with gap between for depth
- No visible floor separations: continuous smooth exterior surface
- Faint star-like surface lights: 10-15 small emissive points embedded in outer shell
- Trailing wisps at edges: 3-5 thin tapering tendrils fading outward from form
- Aurora-like interior glow: muted indigo + silver visible through translucent shells
- Energy thread reception point: barely visible wisp at top where SIA thread arrives
- Underside smooth concave surface reflecting off lake below

### Silhouette Markers
Floating cloud over mirror lake -- the most ethereal form in the city with no geometric edges. Distinguished from Yoga (#02) by having NO domes, NO walkways, and NO visible structure. ## Interior -- Neural Recovery Chamber

### Description
The interior is a continuous flowing space with no hard walls or sharp corners -- every surface curves gently into the next. Recovery pods line the perimeter: translucent cocoon-shaped capsules large enough for one person, each glowing with slow-cycling light that shifts through deep indigo to soft silver, with biometric data displayed as gentle floating symbols above each pod.

A central sleep brain hologram dominates the space: a large translucent brain form showing REM, deep, and light sleep phases as flowing waves of color rather than clinical displays. The entire hologram pulses at 60 BPM -- a visible heartbeat rhythm. Slow particles drift through the space: indigo and silver geometric shapes (small cubes, tetrahedra, spheres) floating at ultra-slow speed like dream fragments suspended in air. Emotional reset nooks are carved into the curved walls: single-person alcoves surrounded by orbiting light points. Breathing walls define the space boundaries -- surfaces that visibly expand and contract on a 4-second cycle in muted indigo, as though the room itself is sleeping.

### Focal Point
The central sleep brain hologram -- a large translucent brain form (approximately 3m wide) pulsing at 60 BPM, with REM/deep/light phases rendered as flowing color waves in indigo, silver, and deep blue.

### Supporting Props (7 items)
- Recovery pods: 4-6 translucent cocoon capsules along perimeter (elongated ellipsoid forms)
- Biometric data symbols above pods: small floating geometric indicators (emissive)
- Dream particles: 15-20 tiny geometric shapes (cubes, tetrahedra) drifting ultra-slow
- Emotional reset nooks: 2 curved wall alcoves with single-seat forms
- Orbiting light points around nooks: 3-4 small emissive spheres per nook
- Breathing wall sections: 2 curved wall panels that reference expansion/contraction cycle
- Floor surface: soft gradient from center (lighter) to edges (darker), seamless curves

### Light Empties
