## Exterior Architecture

### Description
The Fitness Megastructure is built like a clenched fist -- angular, aggressive, muscular. The overall massing features sharp inward angles and jutting cantilevers that give it an imposing physicality unlike any other building in the city. Reinforced steel framework is exposed at the corners and joints, communicating raw structural power. The facade is a grid of large windows framed by heavy dark metal, allowing interior activity to be visible from outside.

Forest green energy strips line every floor edge, creating a horizontal banding pattern that emphasizes the building's stacked, compressed power. At ground level, an outdoor athletic track with embedded green light lanes wraps around the building perimeter. The entrance features dramatic angular framing -- two heavy beams leaning inward to create a triangular entry portal. Rooftop ventilation systems and mechanical housings add industrial texture to the crown. A trophy/achievement display wall near the entrance shows illuminated panels.

### Key Architectural Elements
- Angular main body: 30 floors, sharp inward angles, jutting cantilevers at floors 10/20/30
- Green energy strips on every floor edge (30 horizontal emissive bands)
- Exposed reinforced steel framework at corners and structural joints
- Large windows on each floor (rectangular, framed by heavy dark metal)
- Outdoor athletic track at ground level with embedded green light lanes
- Triangular entrance portal: two heavy angled beams leaning inward
- Rooftop ventilation/mechanical housing (industrial box forms)
- Trophy/achievement display wall near entrance (illuminated panel geometry)
- Energy pipeline connection point on roof (hardpoint for SIA conduit)
- Reinforced base with stepped foundation visible at ground level

### Silhouette Markers
Angular aggressive form that reads as a clenched fist or flexed muscle. The jutting cantilevers at three heights and the inward-angled walls make this the only building with a combative, non-vertical silhouette. Distinctly wider at the middle cantilever than at the base.

## Interior -- Holographic Workout Arena

### Description
A cavernous double-height space spanning floors 1-3, open to a mezzanine level above. The floor is segmented into distinct workout zones: synchronized weightlifting platforms with real-time performance meters floating above each station, an AI-guided treadmill lane with a holographic running partner projected ahead of the runner, and a boxing ring with holographic stats orbiting the perimeter. An upper mezzanine holds a yoga/stretching space overlooking the arena below. Energy pulse floors react to movement with green light ripples. A massive body-performance analytics dashboard dominates the back wall -- a wide curved screen showing aggregate fitness data.

### Focal Point
The massive body-performance analytics dashboard on the back wall -- a wide curved holographic screen (approximately 8m wide, 3m tall) displaying body metrics, workout progress, and district-wide fitness data in green and orange.
