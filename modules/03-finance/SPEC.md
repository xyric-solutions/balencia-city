## Exterior Architecture

### Description
The Finance Tower is a 35-floor crystalline monolith whose facade is composed entirely of faceted surfaces, giving it the appearance of a cut gemstone rising from the earth. The facet angles catch and reflect light differently on every face, creating a prismatic quality no other building possesses. Gold accent lines trace every facet edge, forming a glowing wireframe overlay on the crystalline form that reads clearly even at distance.

The base is deliberately wide and stable -- the widest footprint-to-height ratio of any tower in the city -- communicating financial solidity and unwavering permanence. Large plate glass windows are recessed within the faceted faces, each at a slightly different angle. The crown features a financial data display: a ring of illuminated panels showing abstract market patterns. A reinforced geometric entry at street level uses heavy angular framing with gold-lit edges. A rooftop observation deck with transparent floor panels extends outward from the crown.

### Key Architectural Elements
- Faceted crystalline main body: 35 floors, 8-12 major facet faces, each at unique angle
- Gold accent edges on every facet junction (emissive wireframe effect, 20+ edge lines)
- Wide stable base: hexagonal or octagonal footprint, widest relative base in city
- Large plate glass windows recessed in faceted faces (1-2 per face per floor cluster)
- Crown financial data display: ring of illuminated panel geometry at top 2 floors
- Reinforced geometric entry: heavy angular frame with gold-lit edge accents
- Rooftop observation deck: cantilevered transparent platform extending from crown
- Subtle clock/market display element embedded in entry facade (flat emissive panel)
- Pipeline connection hardpoint at crown level for SIA energy conduit
- Stepped base transition where faceted body meets ground platform

### Silhouette Markers
Faceted gemstone shape -- the only crystalline building in the city. The wide base tapering to a narrower crown through angled facet planes creates a profile unlike any rectangular or organic structure. The gold edge wireframe glow makes it read as a jewel even at thumbnail scale.

## Interior -- Premium Advisory Space

### Description
A refined double-height space occupying floors 1-2, designed as a premium financial advisory environment. AI financial advisor workstations line both sides -- sleek desk forms with holographic projection surfaces above each station showing client portfolio data. The back wall is dominated by the wealth analytics wall: a massive curved display with flowing golden data streams cascading downward like a digital waterfall. Investment holograms -- 3D spinning portfolio visualizations -- float at eye level in the center of the room between the workstation rows. Interactive budget wheels (circular allocation diagrams) hover near each advisor station. A stress-spending correlation display occupies a side alcove, showing abstract wave patterns in gold and red.

### Focal Point
The wealth analytics wall -- a floor-to-ceiling curved display surface on the back wall with cascading golden data streams flowing downward, approximately 10m wide and 6m tall, the single largest visual element in the room.
