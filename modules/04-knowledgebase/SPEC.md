## Exterior Architecture

### Description
The Intelligence Library is an architectural gradient -- ancient wisdom at the base transitioning into futuristic data infrastructure at the crown. The lower 8 floors feature 4-6 massive dark stone columns in a classical temple arrangement, with heavy stone walls and deep-set arched windows that evoke a timeless archive. A visible transition zone at the mid-building level marks where stone gives way to technology: the stone columns taper and merge into sleek metal supports, and the wall surfaces shift from opaque stone to translucent data panels.

The upper half is composed of floating transparent data floors -- glass platforms stacked with visible gaps between them, each floor glowing with royal purple light from within. The purple interior glow is visible through these transparent sections from the outside, making the upper building appear to be made of stacked light. A cascading energy waterfall pours down the front facade from the crown: a visible stream of energy that flows downward rather than being delivered via pipeline, pooling into an energy reservoir at the base. The crown holds a knowledge beacon -- a vertical column of purple light that pulses. The archive vault entrance at ground level is deliberately heavy, secure, and imposing.

### Key Architectural Elements
- Classical stone columns at lower floors: 4-6 large cylindrical columns, fluted optional
- Heavy stone walls at lower floors with deep-set arched windows (8 floors)
- Transition zone at mid-building: stone tapering into metal, texture shift visible
- Floating transparent data floors (upper half): 10-12 glass platforms with gaps between
- Purple interior glow visible through transparent sections (emissive from within)
- Cascading energy waterfall on front facade: vertical stream flowing downward
- Crown knowledge beacon: vertical purple light column above roofline
- Archive vault entrance: heavy door frame, oversized, reinforced, imposing scale
- Exterior data screen panels on upper-floor edges (flat rectangles, holo slot)
- Energy reservoir pool at base of waterfall (glowing ground-level basin)

### Silhouette Markers
Classical stone base transitioning to floating transparent tech top. The only building with this gradient from ancient to futuristic. The visible gap between floating upper floors and the heavy grounded base creates a unique two-part silhouette. The cascading waterfall on the facade adds a vertical flow line no other building has.

## Interior -- Knowledge Archive

### Description
A vast vertical atrium spanning the full building height, surrounded by floating translucent memory cubes -- thousands of small cube forms hovering at various heights in purple and amber tones, each representing stored knowledge. The cubes are semi-transparent and glow faintly, creating a field of luminous data particles throughout the space. Holographic book walls line the perimeter: tall flat surfaces that display abstract book-spine patterns expanding when approached. A central neural learning network visualization dominates the middle space -- a growing 3D knowledge graph with nodes and branching connections that pulse with activity. Knowledge trees rise from the lower floors: vertical structures with data-stream branches and insight leaves made of light. Research chambers are carved into the walls at multiple levels -- alcoves where individual figures are surrounded by AI-synthesized holographic data clouds.

### Focal Point
The central neural learning network visualization -- a massive 3D knowledge graph floating in the atrium center, with pulsing nodes and branching connections that grow and shift, approximately 6m in diameter.

### Supporting Props (7 items)
- Floating memory cubes: hundreds of small cubes at various heights (holo slot, purple + amber)
- Holographic book walls: 3-4 tall flat panels lining perimeter with book-spine patterns
- Knowledge trees: 2-3 vertical branching structures from lower floors (trunk + branch geometry)
- Research chamber alcoves: 3 wall-recessed spaces at different levels with desk forms
- Holographic data clouds in research chambers (small sphere clusters, holo slot)
- Floor-level reading platforms: 2 circular raised platforms with low seating
- Cascading waterfall terminus: interior water-of-light feature where facade waterfall enters
